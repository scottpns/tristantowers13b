The Property

A rare vantage point on the Emerald Coast.

Tristan Towers 13B is one of the most commanding residences on Pensacola Beach. Perched on the 13th floor, this fully renovated 2-bedroom, 2-bath condominium delivers unobstructed panoramic views stretching from the Santa Rosa Sound to the open Gulf of Mexico — a perspective that simply cannot be replicated.

Comprehensively updated in 2024 — new furniture, luxury vinyl plank flooring throughout, granite kitchen surfaces, and smart TVs in every room. Brand new mattresses added November 2024. This unit is completely turnkey. No deferred maintenance, no renovation needed. Walk in and start earning immediately.
